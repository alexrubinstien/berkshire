100 Lightbeam

Vaughn Ontario

Project

Our Lightbeam project in Brampton consists of two (2) new buildings at Steeles Ave and Heritage Rd., with a total of approximately 150,000 sf total of Class ‘A’ industrial space. Building A (10 Lightbeam Terrace), is approximately 80,502sf subdivided into proposed +/- 2780 sf to 4887sf units (24 Units total). Building B (20 Lightbeam Terrace), is approximately 74,723sf subdivided into proposed +/- 5200 sf to 9250 sf units (11 in total). The site accommodates 183 parking spaces, with a saw tooth construction loading area. The Site is zoned Industrial M4.
